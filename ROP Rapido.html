Intoduction

ROP Rapido® is a liquid blend of surfactants, high pressure & friction reducing additives and shale stabilizer designed to increase ROP in lateral section of the well. ROP Rapido® can significantly increases ROP by improving hole cleaning, and preventing the formation of cuttings beds caused by agglomeration. Additionally, ROP Rapido® can reduce ECD and pump pressure to enhance hole stability. This star product delivered extraordinary performance over thousands of wells in the industry.

Applications/Functions

  • ROP Enhancer

  • Widely used in ultra-deep, high-deviated, ERD and long horizontal wells

Advantages

  • Promotes hole cleaning by coating the drilled solids and preventing them from sticking to each other

  • Protects shale formations from hydration and destabilization

  • Effectively reduce ECD

  • Thermally stable to >500ºF

  • Compatible with divalent brines

Typical properties

  • Appearance: Blue Oily Liquid

  • Specific gravity: 0.96

  • Flash Point: >320°F

Recommended Treatment

  • A typical treatment range is 3-6% by volume in water-based mud.
