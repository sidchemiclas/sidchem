Intoduction

SULSCAV速 is a basic zinc carbonate used as a sulfur remover in all kinds of drilling fluid systems. It reacts with sulfide or bisulfide to form insoluble zinc sulfide. SULSCAV速 is effective over a broad pH range. It is recommended that a pH above 10 be maintained when drilling in the formation containing hydrogen sulfide, using this product can remove hydrogen sulfide in time to prevent safety accidents.

Applications/Functions

  • Used to remove sulfides caused by the intrusion of the drilling fluid

Advantages

  • Effectively sulfur removal agent

  • Effective in water-based and oil- or synthetic-base fluids

Typical properties

  • Specific gravity: 3.5

Recommended Treatment
