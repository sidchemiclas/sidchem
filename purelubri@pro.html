Intoduction

PURELUBRI®PRO is completely water dispersible high-performance lubricant for the reduction of torque, drag and potential for differential sticking by decreasing the coefficient of friction in all types of water-based drilling fluids. It has a unique wettability characteristic which lowers the potential for BHA balling. PURELUBRI®PRO is made mainly from vegetable oil and its fatty acid. It contains no hydrocarbons and no harmful to human health and environmental.

Applications/Functions

  • Reduce torque and drag in high angle and S-shaped wells

  • ROP enhancer

  • Contains unique metal-wetting additives which reduce the tendency of soft, sticky shales to cause bit and BHA balling

Advantages

  • Temperatures stable up to 400°F

  • Effective, all-purpose lubricant for all WBM

  • Does not cause foaming

  • Does not cause fluoresce and no impact to logging operation

Typical properties

  • Appearance: Amber clear liquid

  • Specific gravity: 0.95-1.00

  • Flash point:446°F(230℃)

  • pH (0.5% aqueous solution): 7-8

Recommended Treatment

  • A typical range is 3% v/v, but specific drilling conditions will dictate a suitable concentration
