Intoduction

PURELUBRI® is an amine-based additive for the reduction of torque, drag and potential for differential sticking by reducing the coefficient of friction in all types of water-based drilling fluids. It decreases torque and drag thus increasing ROP in WBM, produced water or brine in highly-deviated and horizontal drilling applications. PURELUBRI® is a cost-effective, high-performance lubricant and stable at high pH levels. It is tolerant to high levels of calcium and silicates. PURELUBRI® is biodegradable and does not fluoresce.

Applications/Functions

  • Friction, torque and drag reducer increase ROP

  • Designed for highly-deviated and horizontal land drilling

  • Improves the lubricating qualities of water-based drilling fluids

Advantages

  • Temperatures stable up to 400°F

  • Cost-effective, high-performance lubricant

  • Tolerant to high levels of calcium and silicates

  • Cold environment resistance up to -13°F

Typical properties

  • Appearance: Clear amber liquid

  • Specific gravity: 0.95

  • Flash point: >450°F

Recommended Treatment

  • A typical range is 3% v/v, but specific drilling conditions will dictate a suitable concentration
