Intoduction

SHARKPAC®L filtration control additive is high-quality Polyanionic Cellulose Polymers. It is used in most water-based drilling fluids including fresh water, seawater and brine up to 300°F. SHARKPAC®L has a minimal increase in rheology, is non-toxic and does not require a biocide. SHARKPAC®L is can also encapsulate solids to control dispersion of active shale.

Applications/Functions

  • Control filtration rates with minimal increase in rheology

  • Aids in the formation of a tough, thin filter cake to minimize the potential for differential sticking

Advantages

  • Temperatures stable up to 300°F

  • No fermenting, no biocide needed

  • Effective in fresh water, seawater and brine fluids

  • Functions in all pH range

  • Produces thin, slick, tough filter cake

  • Inhibit hydratable, swelling shales

Typical properties

  • Appearance: Cream-colored, free-flowing powder

  • Specific gravity: 1.5-1.8

  • pH (1% aqueous solution): 7.75

Recommended Treatment

  • A typical treatment range is 0.5-3 ppb
